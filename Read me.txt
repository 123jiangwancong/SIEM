# magnetic and gravity forward modeling programme based on surface integral equation method for Stochastic Intrusive Body 

This repository contains MATLAB code for magnetic and gravity forward modeling, as described in [Forward Modeling of Stochastic Gravity-Magnetic Anomalies from Randomly Realized Heterogeneous Subsurface Models for Deep Learning], *Computers & Geosciences*, 2025.

## Requirements
- MATLAB R2020a or later
- Image Processing Toolbox (optional)
